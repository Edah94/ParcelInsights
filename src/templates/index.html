Generate a PDF Report (Guideline)

With Parcel Insights, you can easily generate PDF reports for your parcel data located in Upper Austria region. Follow these simple steps to generate your parcel-based PDF report:

Map Interaction: Explore the map by clicking on parcels of interest. Parcel details such as KG (parcel administrative unit) and GNR (parcel number) will be automatically extracted. Choose the basemap of your liking for easier navigation.

Manual Entry: Alternatively, you can manually enter the KG and GNR values in the provided input fields.

Generate PDF: After choosing one of two available methods, press the button to initiate the report generation process.
